Recording
USB-C → Mac
Zero hardware needed

Your iPhone is already a great mic.

No need to buy a dedicated microphone. Plug your iPhone into your Mac with a USB-C cable and it works as a high-quality microphone instantly. macOS recognizes it as an audio input device — just select it in Talktiv's settings.

⚡

Pro tip: Plug your iPhone in via USB-C for the best quality and zero latency. It also works wirelessly via Continuity, but wired is more reliable and keeps your phone charged while you work.

Features

Built for developers who think faster than they type.

Hands-free mode

Just speak. Talktiv auto-detects your voice and types it out. No buttons, no shortcuts, no friction.

100% on-device

Whisper AI runs locally. Your voice never touches a server. Zero latency, total privacy, works offline.

Smart cleanup

Filler words, ums, background noise — all stripped automatically. Only clean, polished text gets typed.

Auto-send for terminals

Speak a prompt in iTerm, Terminal, or Warp — Talktiv pastes it and hits Enter automatically. Fully hands-free Claude Code.

Per-app behavior

Configure how Talktiv behaves per app. Auto-send in terminals, paste-only in editors. Fully customizable in Settings.

English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese, and 25+ more. Set a language for faster, more accurate transcription.
