Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Three modes of operation, one app. Voice input, developer tools, and meeting intelligence.

🎤
Voice Input

Speak naturally, get clean text. Talktiv handles the rest.

  • Hands-free mode — voice activity detection auto-starts when you speak. No buttons, no shortcuts.
  • Smart cleanup — filler words, ums, repetition, and background noise stripped automatically.
  • 30+ languages — English, Spanish, French, Japanese, Chinese, and more with automatic detection.
🔨
Developer Tools

Built for the terminal. Speak prompts to Claude, Copilot, or any CLI tool.

  • Auto-send in terminal — speak in iTerm, Terminal, or Warp. Talktiv pastes and hits Enter. Fully hands-free Claude Code.
  • MCP for Claude Code — Claude can search your meetings, find action items, and track context directly from the terminal.
  • Live notepad — voice-first text editor. Compose prompts, emails, docs by speaking. Edit inline, then copy.
📝
Meeting Intelligence

Record any meeting. Get summaries, action items, and decisions automatically.

  • Meeting recording — capture audio from Zoom, Teams, Meet, or in-person. Works with any audio source.
  • Speaker identification — diarization distinguishes who said what, even in group conversations.
  • Auto-extracted insights — summaries, action items, decisions, and key topics pulled from every meeting.
Private by design

Your voice stays
on your Mac.

No accounts. No telemetry. No cloud processing. Talktiv runs entirely on Apple Silicon.

🔒

100% on-device

Whisper AI runs locally on your Mac’s Neural Engine. No API calls, no external servers, no data ever sent.

🔇

Voice never leaves

Your audio is processed in real-time on-device and immediately discarded. Nothing is stored. Nothing is uploaded.

⚡

Works offline

No internet connection required. Talktiv works just as well on a plane, in a cabin, or off the grid.

Recording

Your iPhone is already a great mic.

No need to buy a microphone. Plug your iPhone into your Mac via USB-C and it works as a high-quality audio input instantly. Zero setup.

Pro tip:  Wired USB-C gives best quality + zero latency. Also works wirelessly via Continuity.
